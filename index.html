Tournament Structure

There are 8 pots, each containing 4 teams. Teams in each pot face one another in a league phase to create a league table. After Matchday 3, the top 8 teams in the overall league standings qualify directly to the Round of 16. Teams ranked 9 to 24 enter knockout playoffs, with winners earning the remaining Round of 16 spots. Teams ranked 25 to 32 are eliminated from the tournament.
